As scientists, we know the frustration of open-source tools that are difficult to install and work with — often without a graphical interface — and of ultra-expensive software that is out of reach for most individual users, or even individual laboratories. Our objective is to exploit the advantages of AI-assisted coding to bring high-quality scientific software, designed with laboratory workflows in mind, at a price that individuals can afford.
